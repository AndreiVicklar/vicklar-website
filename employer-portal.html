Back Office

Back office for transport companies.

Vicklar Back Office is the company-side workspace for planners, fleet teams, transport offices and driver operations. It connects the work captured in the Driver App with the office actions needed to manage loads, proof, checks and records.

Company access is reviewed before onboarding. The Hub is intended for genuine transport, logistics, fleet and driver-related businesses.
