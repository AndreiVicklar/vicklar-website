Employer Portal

Verified company workspace for transport operations.

The Vicklar Employer Portal is the company-side workspace direction for transport businesses, planners, fleet teams and driver operations. Company access is not instant public self-service: Vicklar reviews each employer request before creating a workspace.

Company access

How employer onboarding works

Employer Portal access is opened through a company request, not a public sign-up button. This protects the platform and keeps access focused on genuine transport, logistics, fleet and driver-related businesses.

  1. Send a company access request to Vicklar.
  2. Vicklar checks the company context and transport relevance.
  3. We discuss the required modules, portal users and connected drivers.
  4. Vicklar creates the company workspace and admin access.
  5. Your office team can then onboard planners, managers and drivers in a controlled way.

Pricing

Quoted for each company setup

Employer Portal pricing is quoted after onboarding review, because the correct package depends on company size, portal users, connected drivers, modules, support needs and rollout scope.

A typical quote can include a workspace fee, portal-user access and connected-driver access. Pilot pricing, larger fleet pricing and custom employer setups are handled directly with Vicklar.

Future versions may support more automated company onboarding, but the current approach is manual review first.
