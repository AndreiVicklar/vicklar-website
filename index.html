Platform direction

For drivers and transport offices.

Vicklar is built as a connected product package: the driver app captures work in the field, while the Back Office helps the office review, organise and act on the same operational data.

D

Driver records

Digital logbook, documents, tacho slip photo scan, checks, expenses, fuel, timesheets and agency pack reports.

L

Loads & dispatch

Loads, stop sequences, vehicle evidence, references, mileage fallback, collection and delivery status.

P

POD & proof

Delivery proof, signatures, damage photos, odometer details, load height and regenerable POD evidence from the office.

✓

Vehicle checks

Daily, weekly and tyre checks for employer fleet vehicles, routed to the company workspace instead of personal driver cloud history.

F

Fleet & storage direction

Fleet records, vehicle templates and storage/compound workflows form part of the wider back-office roadmap.

C

Cloud continuity

Plan-based cloud backup, restore and company workspace features are designed for a scalable foundation, with internal operations tooling kept separate from customer-facing products.

Company access

Back Office access is reviewed before onboarding.

Company workspaces are not open public self-service accounts. Vicklar reviews transport, logistics, fleet and driver-related businesses before enabling back-office access.
