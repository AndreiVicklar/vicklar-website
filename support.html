Support

Contact Vicklar for website questions, product enquiries, support requests, account matters, or privacy-related requests.

Support contact: office@vicklar.com

How to contact us

Please email office@vicklar.com and use a clear subject line so your request can be identified quickly.

  • General enquiry: website or company questions
  • Product enquiry: Vicklar Driver information
  • Support request: technical or account-related help
  • Privacy request: data access, correction, or deletion matters
  • Billing enquiry: subscription or payment-related questions

Useful information to include

  • your full name;
  • the email address linked to your enquiry or account;
  • device type and operating system, if the issue is technical;
  • a short description of the issue;
  • screenshots or relevant details where helpful.

Response times

Vicklar aims to respond within a reasonable timeframe. Response times may vary depending on message volume, weekends, public holidays, or the complexity of the issue.

Security note

Do not send passwords, payment card details, or unnecessary sensitive personal information by email. We may ask you to verify your identity before acting on account or privacy requests.

Account and deletion requests

For account closure or personal data deletion requests, please also review the Delete Account page.
