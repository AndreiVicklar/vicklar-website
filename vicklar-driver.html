Vicklar Driver

Enterprise app for modern professional drivers.

Vicklar Driver is an enterprise-grade driver app available now for individual professional drivers.

Built for modern transport work, it helps drivers replace paper notes, scattered photos and manual records with a cleaner digital workflow for logbook, documents, fuel, expenses, checks, timesheets and tacho slip photo scan.

Designed, tested and improved with real driver feedback, Vicklar Driver is made for daily road use, not just office theory.

Built for professional drivers

For real work on the road.

Vicklar Driver is designed for professional drivers who need a clearer way to organise daily work without relying on loose notes, folders and repeated manual paperwork.

The app is suitable for HGV drivers, car transporter drivers, van drivers, agency drivers and professional drivers working across the UK and Europe in the first stage.

The platform foundation is built to support wider international expansion after the Driver version is stabilised.

What the app brings together

One place for daily driver records.

Vicklar Driver includes a practical base for logbook entries, weekly summaries, timesheets, agency pack reports, pay calculator, documents, contacts, fuel, expenses, daily checks, weekly checks, loads, photo records and tacho slip photo scan.

It is a local-first driver workflow with optional cloud continuity on Pro for backup, restore and encrypted document storage.

Privacy by design

Only capture what the feature needs.

Photo import, camera and location features are used for user-facing workflows such as tacho slip photo scan, document photos and “Find me” location filling. Vicklar does not use these features for hidden background tracking.

Important usage note

Vicklar Driver is an administration and record-organisation tool. It does not replace legal responsibilities, employer instructions, official tachograph records, statutory compliance checks or professional judgement. Drivers and employers remain responsible for verifying any scanned, imported or manually entered information.
