Account and privacy

Delete account

Use this page to request deletion of a Vicklar account and associated personal data.

Last updated: 24 April 2026

How to request deletion

Email office@vicklar.com with the subject line Vicklar account deletion request.

  • Include the email address used for your Vicklar account.
  • Tell us whether you want account deletion only or account + associated app/cloud data deletion.
  • Do not send passwords or unnecessary sensitive documents by email.
Email deletion request

Identity verification

For security, we may ask you to verify that you control the account email address before we delete account data. We may refuse or delay deletion where required by law, fraud prevention, security or unresolved billing/dispute obligations.

Typical response route

We review the request, verify account ownership where necessary and confirm what data can be deleted.

Data usually deleted

  • account profile data linked to the Vicklar account;
  • cloud backup/sync data linked to the account, where applicable;
  • user-created app records, documents, images and operational entries stored in Vicklar cloud, where applicable;
  • driver CV data and visibility settings linked to the account, where applicable.

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained for legal compliance, accounting, fraud prevention, security logs, dispute handling, backup integrity or legitimate business records.

Local app data

Deleting an account from Vicklar systems may not automatically erase data stored locally on a device. You may need to use the app’s reset/delete options or uninstall the app to remove local data from the device.
