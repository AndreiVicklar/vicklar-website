Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how VICKLAR LTD collects, uses, stores, protects and shares personal data in connection with the Vicklar website, Vicklar Driver mobile app, Employer Portal, cloud features, support and related services.

Unless stated otherwise, VICKLAR LTD is the controller for personal data processed through our website, mobile app, cloud features and support communications.

2. Services covered

  • the Vicklar website;
  • Vicklar Driver mobile app;
  • cloud backup, restore, sync and account features;
  • Employer Portal and employer workspace features where enabled;
  • support, account, deletion, billing and product communications.

3. Data we collect or process

  • Account and identity data: name, email, account identifiers, login/session information, plan/entitlement status and device registration data.
  • Driver profile and CV data: driver name, licence categories, experience, sectors, languages, right-to-work information, visibility setting and employer-facing CV fields.
  • Logbook and operational data: daily entries, dates, times, rest, driving, other work, POA, locations, notes, vehicle registrations, mileage and summaries.
  • Tacho slip photo scan data: camera frames or photos chosen by the user, extracted text/values and confirmed scan results.
  • Document data: document type, expiry date, document photos/images and related notes that you choose to add.
  • Checks, fuel and expenses: vehicle check records, defect photos, fuel entries, expense entries, receipts, locations and notes.
  • Location data: location information only when you use features such as “Find me” to fill a location field. Vicklar does not use hidden continuous background tracking for this purpose.
  • Contacts data: contacts that you manually add into the app or choose to store for your own workflow.
  • Employer workspace data: company membership, role/access information, employer loads, templates, messages, shared records and portal activity where applicable.
  • Support and technical data: emails, screenshots, issue descriptions, device type, operating system, app version, IP address, diagnostics, crash/error logs and service logs.
  • Billing metadata: plan status, purchase tokens/receipt references and entitlement records. Full card details are normally processed by app stores or payment providers.

4. Camera, photos and media

Vicklar Driver may request camera or photo/media access for tacho slip photo scan, taking document photos, importing slip photos, receipt photos, defect evidence and attaching images to records.

Camera and photo features are used to provide app functionality. We do not sell camera, photo or media data and we do not use them for third-party advertising. Some photos or extracted values may remain local to your device or may be uploaded to Vicklar cloud if you use cloud backup/sync or employer-connected features that require it.

5. How we use data

  • to provide the app, website, portal, cloud sync/restore and support services;
  • to create, store, display, back up and restore user records;
  • to process tacho slip photo scans and pre-fill logbook values for user confirmation;
  • to manage accounts, authentication, device access, plan entitlements and billing status;
  • to support employer workspace features where a driver and employer relationship exists;
  • to respond to support, privacy, account and deletion requests;
  • to secure the service, prevent abuse, debug errors and maintain reliability;
  • to comply with legal obligations and protect rights, safety and legitimate business interests.

6. Lawful bases

Where UK GDPR or similar laws apply, we rely on one or more lawful bases, including contract performance, legitimate interests, legal obligation and consent where required.

7. Sharing of data

We may share data with service providers only where reasonably necessary to run the service, such as cloud hosting, storage, database, security, email, app-store, payment, analytics/diagnostics and support providers. We may also share data where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use user data for third-party advertising.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards, including encrypted transport where applicable, access controls, account/device protections and secure cloud infrastructure. Certain document cloud features may use additional encryption controls depending on product plan and implementation. No digital service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Retention and deletion

We keep data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain account records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and support backups. User-created operational records and documents are normally retained until the user deletes them, the account is deleted, or retention is no longer required for the service.

To request deletion, use the Delete Account page or contact office@vicklar.com.

10. Account deletion

If you have a Vicklar account, you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing office@vicklar.com or using the Delete Account page. We may need to verify your identity first.

11. International transfers

Some infrastructure or service providers may process data outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards and contractual controls.

12. Children

Vicklar services are designed for professional driving and transport operations and are not directed to children.

13. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability or withdrawal of consent. Contact office@vicklar.com.

14. Cookies and website technologies

The website may use essential technical mechanisms needed for security, functionality and performance. If optional analytics or tracking technologies are introduced materially, this policy and any required consent mechanisms will be updated.

15. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The latest version published on this website applies from the stated update date.
